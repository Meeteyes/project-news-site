The performance LATVIAN LOVE is based on true events and actors’ own fantasies. Materials obtained during interviews and meetings – personal stories, letters, photographs – served as a source for the actors’ fantasy and work on the parts. The performance consists of 13 scenes, each presenting different dating situation and social group. All the characters represent some true to type personalities one can find somewhere next to us either in Riga or in country-side.
